1. Shipping — Merchandise

All merchandise orders (T-shirts, accessories, posters, postcards and other physical products) are dispatched from Brisbane, Queensland. We ship Australia-wide using Australia Post tracked services.

Order Value Shipping Method Cost Estimated Delivery
Under $100 Australia Post Standard $10.00 flat rate 3–7 business days
$100 or over Australia Post Standard FREE 3–7 business days
Any value Australia Post Express $18.00 1–3 business days
Free shipping threshold: Orders totalling $100 or more (after any discounts) qualify for free standard shipping automatically at checkout. No coupon code required.

International Shipping

At this time, we are only able to ship merchandise within Australia. We hope to offer international shipping in the future. If you are located outside Australia and wish to purchase, please contact us to discuss options.

2. Shipping — Professional Resources

Physical professional resources (booklets, pamphlets and print materials) are listed at $0.00 as they are provided free of charge. However, a flat shipping fee applies to cover postage and handling costs.

Resource Type Product Cost Shipping Fee Notes
Physical Booklets & Print Materials $0.00 $10.00 flat rate Shipped directly to your service address
Digital PDF Resources $0.00 FREE Instant download after order confirmation
Order limits apply. To ensure equitable access across all Queensland services, we place limits on the quantity of physical resources that can be ordered online. If you require a bulk order, please contact our Education and Community Engagement Team at ed@childrenbychoice.org.au.

Resource Delivery Address

Physical resources are shipped directly to your nominated service or clinic address. Please ensure your delivery address, phone number and email are entered correctly at checkout so our team can contact you if needed.

3. Delivery Times

All orders are processed within 1–2 business days of payment confirmation. Orders placed on weekends or public holidays will be processed on the next business day.

Once dispatched, you will receive a shipping confirmation email containing your Australia Post tracking number. Delivery timeframes are estimates only and may vary during peak periods (e.g. Christmas, Easter).

Processing Delays

During high-demand periods or if stock levels require a reorder, processing times may extend to 3–5 business days. We will notify you by email if your order is subject to any delay.

4. Returns & Exchanges

We want you to love your purchase. If you are not completely satisfied, we offer returns and exchanges under the following conditions.

Eligibility

  • Items must be returned within 30 days of the delivery date.
  • Items must be unworn, unwashed and in their original condition with tags attached.
  • Items must be returned in their original packaging where possible.
  • Sale items and items marked as final sale are not eligible for return or exchange.

How to Initiate a Return

  1. Email shop@childrenbychoice.org.au with your order number and reason for return.
  2. Our team will respond within 2 business days with a return authorisation and instructions.
  3. Pack the item securely and send it to the address provided in the return authorisation.
  4. Once received and inspected, we will process your refund or exchange within 5 business days.

Return Shipping Costs

Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item is faulty, damaged or incorrectly sent. In those cases, we will provide a prepaid return label.

Refunds

Approved refunds will be credited to your original payment method. Please allow 5–10 business days for the refund to appear, depending on your bank or payment provider.

Note on professional resources: Physical resources are provided free of charge and are not eligible for return or exchange. If you received incorrect or damaged resources, please contact ed@childrenbychoice.org.au.

5. Digital Downloads

Digital PDF resources are available for immediate download following order confirmation. Download links are provided in your order confirmation email and are also accessible via your My Account portal under the Downloads tab.

Download Access

Download links remain active for 12 months from the date of purchase. If your link has expired or you are experiencing difficulties downloading, please contact us and we will reissue access.

Refunds on Digital Products

As digital products are delivered immediately upon purchase, we are unable to offer refunds once a download has been accessed. If you experience a technical issue with a file, please contact us and we will resolve it promptly.

6. Damaged or Lost Orders

If your order arrives damaged or does not arrive within the estimated delivery window, please contact us as soon as possible so we can investigate and resolve the issue.

Damaged Items

If your item arrives damaged, please email shop@childrenbychoice.org.au with your order number and clear photos of the damage within 7 days of delivery. We will arrange a replacement or refund at no additional cost.

Lost in Transit

If your tracking shows no movement for more than 10 business days, or your order has not arrived within 14 business days of dispatch, please contact us. We will lodge an investigation with Australia Post on your behalf and keep you updated throughout the process.

Need Help With Your Order?

Our team is here to help. Get in touch and we'll resolve your query as quickly as possible.
