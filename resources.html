Frequently Asked Questions

Many of our resources are the direct result of grant projects and activities within our Education and Community Engagement Team. Unfortunately, we have limited capacity to stock physical resources, and to ensure all services are able to receive CbyC resources we have put limits on our online requests. If you would like to make a bulk order, please contact our team at ed@childrenbychoice.org.au.

All our resources are free of charge. Depending on the type of resource you order, there may be shipping fees attached to keep up with our high capacity. If you have any questions or clarifications about shipping costs, please contact us at ed@childrenbychoice.org.au.

There's no need for pick-up, we ship resource requests directly to services! Please add your preferred address, phone number, and email on your request so we can contact you if needed.

Please contact our Education and Community Engagement Team at ed@childrenbychoice.org.au or call us on (07) 3357 9933 for more information about our resources.
